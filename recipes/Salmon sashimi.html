Salmon sashimi

Description

Sashimi is a Japanese delicacy consisting of fresh raw fish or meat sliced into thin pieces and often eaten with soy sauce

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Salmon sashimi
  • Mirin
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Garlic
  • Ponzu
  • Lb salmon sashimi sushi grade
  • Onion
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Yuzu juice
  • Cucumber
  • Ginger
  • Rice
  • Scallion

Steps

  1. Cut an even piece of the salmon fillet and cut into 3 mm thin slices. Diagonally cut the fillet, always against the marbling in the fish.
  2. Thinly slice the red radish and cut the white radish into thin strips.
  3. Mix salmon roe with wasabi.
  4. Juice lime and mix with soy sauce.
  5. Serve the sashimi with radish, salmon roe, sesame seeds and coriander over, and drizzle the soy sauce around.
